Walking

Walking

All about our Walking services.

Walking each day is vital to living a long and happy life. Sasha's Paw Spa can walk your dog and/or facilitate a midday potty break while you're at work. Contact Sasha to arrange an initial meet & greet.


Initial Meet & Greet, $1000 Sasha will visit your home and get acquainted with your pet/s. You'll also discuss your needs (regular walks or as scheduled) and pick up keys, if necessary.


Dog Walk, $1000/hour A 30 minute or 1 hour walk. Depending on your location and pet's temperament, other dogs may join. If you have multiple dogs, each additional dog can be added at 50% of the normal rate.

Grooming

Grooming

All about our grooming services.

Prices for full-service baths and grooms are based on pet's weight and breed or fur/hair length. They are displayed for small/medium/large dogs. Generally, a small dog is any dog under 10 lbs, medium under 40 lbs, and large is over 60 lbs. Your dog's category is up to Sasha's discretion.


Grooming Services:

  • Bath $60/70/80
  • Haircut or trim $30/40/50
  • Deshedding
  • Nail trimming
  • Teeth brushing
  • Gland expression


Full Service Grooming, $200/300/400 Includes natural shampoo, blow-dry, ear cleaning, gland expression, 15-minute brush-out, scented spritz & nail trim.


Full-service dog bath with haircut, $400/500/600 Includes natural shampoo, blow-dry, ear cleaning, gland expression, 15-minute brush-out, scented spritz & nail trim, PLUS cut & style, including to breed-specific standard.

Training

Training

All about our Training services.


As veteran trainers, we know that obedience is a two-way street. We offer in-home sessions and train both the dog and its owner in their natural habitat. We specialize in all levels of dog training, including puppy training, basic obedience, and intense behavior modification. We can even help dogs who seem to be helpless.
